About us

Our Story

Our roots go back to the year 1948, i.e., 3 generations back, when South India’s first private school was established at Mangalore. The past 2 decades have witnessed the world changing faster than ever with far-reaching technological advancements like artificial intelligence and extraordinary global challenges like climate-change.

As educationists with decades of experience behind us, this has prompted us to introspect and question ourselves whether the prevailing schooling system is preparing today’s children to navigate a changed tomorrow.

It was these thoughts which have sowed the seeds of creating a set of world-class progressive K-12 schools, which will equip students with the right skills, mindsets, and global exposure necessary to thrive as global citizens of tomorrow. Thus, born TCIS in the year 2016. Today there are 2 TCIS schools, both instituere per excellentiam.

Our Philosophy

We always dreamt things that never were and said ‘why not’. We wanted to make a difference in the lives of children by challenging stereotypical beliefs about intelligence, ability and academic excellence; for we believe all students are capable of unique efforts that would lead to better outcomes.

At TCIS, the learner is at the centre of our universe! TCIS fosters an atmosphere where all students feel they are valued, and their individuality is recognized and nurtured by providing a non-threatening and joyful learning atmosphere. We build a sense of community in the classroom with each learner at its centre. Our outcome based approach is aimed at creating well-grounded, successful, competitive, global citizens combining academic excellence with creative mindedness and physical & emotional wellbeing.

TCIS is dedicated to nurturing responsible global citizens with academic brilliance and co-scholastic excellence. We reimagin education to equip children with lifelong global skills—both academic and co-scholastic—helping them navigate an ever-changing world. While the TCIS approach has garnered numerous accolades, its effectiveness is most evident in the accomplishments of its students and alumni.

Our People

What makes TCIS different is its quality people, from teachers to admin to management, coming from diverse background, bringing together a plethora of experiences and skill sets, all working towards a common course. In order to encourage quality through cross-pollination of skills and mindsets, in a pioneering move TCIS has professionalised its non-academic team with >80% recruited from MNCs and large corporates.
