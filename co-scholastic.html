    TCIS

    Co-Scholastic

    Co-Scholastic

    Researches have underlined that sports, theatre, music, visual arts and story-telling are powerful brain tools which impart crucial skills for children to excel in academics and life at large.

    TCIS views co-scholastics as not merely extra-curricular activities, but serious learning tools. It has developed well-structured curriculamised program, integrated with academic learning, for Sports, Performing Arts, Visual Arts, Value Ed, STEM, Entrepreneurship and Literature, which is grade appropriate, cutting across the school.

    STEM @ TCIS:

    Empowering Curiosity

    In the realm of STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics), TCIS has created an innovative program partnering with STEMx India and KTC Holdings Japan, bringing cutting-edge technology into the classroom.

    From motor mechanics to coding IoT devices, students engage in hands-on projects that make theoretical knowledge practical. The STEM lab opens doors to fields like robotics, astronomy, and environmental science, sparking curiosity and preparing students for the future.

    Fostering Entrepreneurship

    At TCIS entrepreneurship is considered as a life-skill. Our entrepreneurship program starts as early as Grade-3 and turns much immersive as it reaches higher grades with opportunities ranging from internships to real-life start-up ideas pitched to eminent investors.

    Students get live exposure with global platforms like UQ Ventures of University of Queensland Australia, India Future Founders Competition, etc.

    World Class

    Sports Program

    TCIS offers a well-rounded sports education enabled by its world-class sports infrastructure and national/state champion coaches. The school has consistently produced outstanding sports talents who have represented India in prestigious events such as the Commonwealth Games and International Children Olympics. By combining sports science with a comprehensive curriculum, TCIS ensures that students not only excel academically but also on the field.

    For Students

    Cultivating Love for Music

    Love For Music

    Through its partnership with globally acclaimed music institutes, the school offers students access to curricularised, age-appropriate world-class musical education. From advanced musical theory to systematic coaching, TCIS encourages students to explore their talents and even supports those interested in pursuing music education with institutions like Trinity College London and Berklee College of Music.
