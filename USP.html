Child-centric Differentiated Learning

Child-at-the-centre approach is in our DNA. The entire teaching strategy is decided based on the learning needs of the child. This teaching-learning is facilitated by a two prone strategy adopted by TCIS, firstly building a solid grade-appropriate foundation in literacy, numeracy, scientific temper, design thinking and innovative problem solving skills, and secondly, providing a personalised learning environment and infrastructure that supports and motivates each student to nurture her aptitude and passion.

Academic & Co-scholastic Accomplishments

The prowess of TCIS’s unique approach is evident in its students’ accomplishments. In addition to consistently achieving 100% pass rates, an impressive 85% of students secure distinctions in the Grade 10 and Grade 12 Board exams each year. Furthermore, every TCIS student successfully gains admission to their chosen path after the school, whether in professional fields, research, or entrepreneurship. Notably, 30% of its graduates gain admission to prestigious institutions across the country each year, including IITs, NITs, NIDs, and IISERs, while another 20% secure places at top universities abroad, serving as a testament to the effectiveness of TCIS's academic strategy.
